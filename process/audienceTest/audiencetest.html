Audience Test

    TASKS

    1. Click through links: interests/emphasis
    2. Read through main interest description
    3. Scan the page for possible careers under specific interest
    4. Click and expand on specific career for details

    QUESTIONS

    1. Do you know the purpose of this site right when you enter it?
    2. Do the overarching interests/categories cover the design department's emphases?
    3. Do the overarching interests/categories cover YOUR design emphasis?
    4. Is expanding the descriptions of each topic/career intuitive?
    5. After finding topics/careers of your interest, would you move foward to do more research on your own?
    6. Would the resource links (more detailed links to each career, links to job hunting sites, etc.) be helpful?
    7. Is the organization of the click through links/pages intuitive?
    8. Are you confused at all at any point of using this site?
