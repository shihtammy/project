Career Exploration Tool

Hi there and welcome to the Design Career Exploration Tool! The UC Davis Design Department has created this tool to help you explore your career options and see how you can turn your passion for design into a career. Don't know where to start or don't know what fields and careers exist in the design world? It's okay! The design field is HUGE. This tool will help you discover and narrow down possible industries and careers you may be interested in pursuing in the near future based off of the classes you have taken, here at UC Davis.

How to begin: Based off of the design classes you've taken here at UC Davis, which ones did you enjoy the most? Which ones are you interested in pursuing futher? Make note of those classes and let's get exploring!
